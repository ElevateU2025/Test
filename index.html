🌍 The Global Gameplan – Youth Delegates Challenge

(part of ElevateU 2025)

This isn't your average debate. This is where YOU step into the shoes of world leaders 🌎.

⚡ What's It About?

The Global Gameplan is a unique international-style challenge designed for Class 11 & 12 students.

🌍 Act as youth delegates representing any country in the world
💡 Think like global leaders → bold, original, and actionable solutions
📝 Submit your ideas independently before the event
🎓 Three days before ElevateU event day → you'll be paired into teams, given a country, and the best teams present live before a panel of top university professors at ElevateU

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in ElevateU 2025?

+

Only regular Class 11 & 12 students can participate. Dummy school students and gap-year students are not allowed.

Is there any registration fee?

+

No! Entry is completely FREE. However, registration is mandatory as seats are limited.

What should I expect at ElevateU?

+

Hands-on workshops, youth competitions, university meet-and-greets, career exploration, networking opportunities, and a live band performance!

How do I register for the event?

+

Click on any of the "Register Now" buttons on this page. Registration is mandatory and seats are limited, so register early!

What competitions are available?

+

We have two main competitions: The Global Gameplan – Youth Delegates Challenge and Fund a Future. Both are designed specifically for Class 11 & 12 students.
